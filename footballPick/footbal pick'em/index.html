Here We Go!

Welcome to the family football pick'em game. This is your chance to pick against other family members and show your knowledge or luck with picking the winning football team.

The Rules

The rules are simple pick the team you think will win each of the current weeks matchups by originally scheduled kick off time.

  1. Picks are made by selecting the checkbox by each team name.
  2. Each win will be counted.
  3. Person with the most wins at the end of the playoffs gets winner trophy.
  4. Person with the least wins at the end of the playoffs gets the losers trophy
  5. Profile is required to play.
